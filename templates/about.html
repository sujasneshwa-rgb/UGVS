UNIVERSITY GRIEVANCE VOTING SYSTEM

The University Grievance Voting System is a centralized digital platform designed to streamline and modernize the grievance management process within educational institutions. The system enables students to formally submit, track, and participate in the resolution of grievances at both college and university levels in a structured and transparent manner. This platform addresses common issues related to academics, infrastructure, examinations, hostel facilities, and administrative services. By digitizing the grievance submission process, the system eliminates manual paperwork, reduces delays, and ensures that student concerns are documented and handled efficiently. A key feature of UGVS is its voting mechanism, which allows students to support grievances raised by their peers. This democratic approach helps prioritize issues based on collective concern. Grievances receiving higher votes are given greater attention, ensuring that the most pressing matters are addressed promptly by the concerned authorities.
